ABOUT DESIGN ITUS

DESIGN ITUS club was established on October 25, 2010, is an academic club of the Faculty of Information Technology, Vietnam National University, Ho Chi Minh City, University of Science.
The club is a place for students with passion for graphic design, UI/UX design, photography, photo editing, video making and editing, hand-drawing and digital painting.
