Bryd Hall

The Space Expo, an annual student-run event highlighting recent developments in space sciences and astronomy, is coming to the Center for Space Science and Engineering, Friday, April 24 to Sunday, April 26.

Bryd Hall will be the site of several special events, including "Students Day", in which local area students compete for science awards. Representatives from industry will display the latest results of their research over the weekend.

Call 555-9801 for more event calendar and registration information.
